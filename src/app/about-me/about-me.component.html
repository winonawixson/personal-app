Hi! I'm Winona Wixson, a software developer at HMB in Columbus, OH. My career in software development was a bit unconventional. I started by graduating with a degree in mathematics and went on to attend a software bootcamp, The Software Guild. Soon after, I landed in Columbus at a healthcare startup where I had to learn .Net on the fly and learn the ropes of startup life. My time there really helped me kickstart my career as I was given lots of opportunities to experience all aspects of the company. Most of my time was spent coding, but I dabbled in creating requirements, qa-ing, troubleshooting issues for customers and reporting feature statuses to the CEO.

After a year there, I decided it was time for a change to learn some new technologies and business domains as well as settle down on work hours. Consulting seemed to be the right choice for me and I landed at HMB where I've continued to grow as a developer and a professional. So far, my favorite project has been an Ionic/Angular mobile app for a sales team. I'm excited to see where this adventure takes me!
